### Step 2: Deploy to Netlify (Recommended - Easiest)

**Why Netlify?**
- Free for personal sites
- Automatic HTTPS
- Custom domain support
- Instant deploys
- No server management

**Steps:**

1. **Go to** [netlify.com](https://netlify.com) and sign up (free)

2. **Drag & Drop Deploy:**
   - Click "Add new site" â†’ "Deploy manually"
   - Drag your entire site folder into the upload area
   - Wait 30 seconds - your site is live!

3. **Connect Your Domain:**
   - In Netlify dashboard, go to "Domain settings"
   - Click "Add custom domain"
   - Enter: `wolstenstudios.com`
   - Follow the DNS instructions to point your domain to Netlify
   - HTTPS will auto-configure in ~1 hour

4. **Done!** Your site is live at `wolstenstudios.com`
